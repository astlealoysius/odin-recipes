Aloo Gobi

Description

Aloo Gobi is a popular dry Indian dish made with potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi) cooked with Indian spices. It's a simple yet flavorful vegetarian dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 medium cauliflower (cut into florets)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add potatoes, turmeric, and salt. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add cauliflower florets and tomatoes. Cover and cook until tender.
  5. Add garam masala and coriander powder. Stir well.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with roti or paratha.
