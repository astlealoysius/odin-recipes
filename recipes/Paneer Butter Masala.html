Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy and rich dish where paneer (Indian cottage cheese) is cooked in a smooth, buttery tomato-based gravy. It's a popular vegetarian dish served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 medium onions (finely chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Stir well.
  5. Pour cream and sprinkle kasuri methi. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
