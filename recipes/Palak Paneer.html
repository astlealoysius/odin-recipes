Palak Paneer

Description

Palak Paneer is a healthy and delicious dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a smooth spinach gravy, flavored with Indian spices.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 2 cups spinach leaves (blanched and pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add spinach puree and garam masala. Stir well.
  6. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Optionally, add cream for a richer taste. Serve hot with naan or rice.
