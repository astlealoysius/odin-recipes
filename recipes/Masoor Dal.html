Masoor Dal

Description

Masoor Dal is a comforting Indian lentil soup made with red lentils (masoor dal) cooked with onions, tomatoes, and aromatic Indian spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils (masoor dal)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Chopped cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse lentils and cook them with turmeric and salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add the cooked lentils and garam masala. Stir well.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or chapati.
